About Regonna

Regonna is an innovative platform for auctioning train tickets during peak hours. We offer a unique solution to the problem of ticket scarcity by allowing users to bid on available seats.

Our auction system operates daily from 1 PM to 3 PM, providing a fair chance for all travelers to secure their tickets.
